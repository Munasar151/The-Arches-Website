Arches Community Centre

"Education is all a matter of building bridges. Ellison, R"










The Arches is a local company teaching the local community computing skills. They have target audience for the site is local people who wish to take training courses in a local setting. They continually strive to ensure that they provide the local community the chance at the best education, training and learning opportunities.

In June 2019 they opened our new Education & Learning Centre in Digbeth. Known locally as the arches it is a modern facility within an historic building – an ideal place for the education and training.

The team of learning organizers work closely with the local community members, individuals, employers and key stakeholders to create and deliver learning solutions, in line with community’s values of being ‘The modern union for a changing world’.

They believe that access to learning is an essential human right. Education builds confidence and self-esteem, and gives people pride. It’s also key to succeeding in our changing working world, where flexible skill development is becoming vital.

The Centre

  • Wheelchair access throughout the venue.
  • 1 fully equipped training room (capacity x 20 computer machines)
  • On-site parking for 50 vehicles
  • 24/7 free internet access available at Arches Cybercafé
  • Onsite catering and coffee bar
