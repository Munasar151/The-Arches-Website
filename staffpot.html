Nadia Kaur - Learning Developer, IT
📧 kaur.n@dlc.org.uk

My role as an educator and the need to keep learning is very important to me. I taught for ten years, three years I have been teaching Adult Learners, which is incredibly rewarding and humbling. I too was an Adult Learner; I studied for an M.A. in Computing and Education on a part time basis, juggling a busy job as well as keeping time to be there for my family. In my spare time I undertake school residencies using technology as a tool to energize children and unleash their creativity. I was recently involved with students setting up a business to print on to different materials designs that they had made. They successfully raised over £20,000.
